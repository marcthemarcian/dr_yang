DR Yang

What's it for?

DR Yang is a web application powered by machine learning algorithms like SVM and Naive Bayes. DR Yang is a heart problem predictor that takes in a patient's data and tries to classify whether the patient has a presence of heart problem.

This application is driven by the passion to help cardiologists in making smarter and faster diagnosis on their patients. With DR Yang, doctors can reassure their patients that their diagnoses are supported by a smart application fueled by big data from all over the world.
